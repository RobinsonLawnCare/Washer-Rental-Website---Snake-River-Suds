Local. Reliable. Snake River Suds.

Based in Southeast Idaho, we saw a problem: moving into a new apartment or starting a family is expensive enough without a $1,500 appliance bill. We founded Snake River Suds to provide a low-barrier, high-service rental option for our neighbors. We manage the machines so you can manage your life.

Common Questions

What if the machine breaks?

Maintenance is 100% free. If we can't fix it on-site immediately, we do a same-day emergency swap.

Is there a credit check?

No. We believe in accessible service. We just require a valid ID and a signed 6-month minimum agreement.

Can I buy the units later?

Yes! Ask us about our Lease-to-Own options that transfer ownership after 18-24 months.
